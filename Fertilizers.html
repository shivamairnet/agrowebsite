Fertilizers

In addition to our petrochemical portfolio, we excel in exporting a wide range of fertilizers across Africa. Our focus includes supplying high-quality Urea, predominantly 46%, in granular and prilled form. Leveraging our robust supplier network, we source products tailored to our clients’ needs, including fertilizer derivatives such as NPK, MAP, DAP, and Ammonia derivatives, fostering agricultural sustainability in Africa and beyond.

Our Diverse Fertilizers Product Portfolio

Urea - At Simpra Foods Goods Wholesalers, we offer a comprehensive range of urea products tailored to meet diverse industry needs. Our selection includes granular urea, prilled urea, and specialized urea formulations designed for agricultural and industrial applications.

NPK - At Simpra Foods Goods Wholesalers, we offer a diverse range of NPK products tailored to meet the specific needs of agriculture and horticulture sectors. Our selection includes various formulations of nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K), designed to optimize plant growth, improve soil fertility, and enhance crop yields. From balanced NPK blends suitable for general applications to specialized formulations for specific crops and soil conditions, our products ensure high quality and consistent performance.

Ammonia - At Simpra Foods Goods Wholesalers, we offer a diverse range of high-quality Ammonia products tailored to meet various industrial and commercial needs. Our portfolio includes anhydrous ammonia, aqueous ammonia solutions, and ammonia gas, each meticulously sourced and processed to ensure exceptional purity and effectiveness.
